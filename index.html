Restaurant Week Map no longer available

Thank you for visiting Madison's Restaurant Week Map! We had lots of fun creating it over the years, but the time has come to put it to rest. Madison Magazine has decided to assert its rightful copyright on the event, and it just didn't make sense to continue development or content updates. We're thankful for the volunteers who put in the time to make this site great, and we especially thank Madison Magazine for putting on such a stellar event.

Check out the official Restaurant Week pages to see your favorite restaurants' participation.
